## TERRAWAVE STYLE BIBLE v1.0 – Audit & Cleanup Plan

### 1. Redundancies

| Lines/Ranges                                                                 | Issue                                                                         | Recommendation                                                               |
| ---------------------------------------------------------------------------- | ----------------------------------------------------------------------------- | ---------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Entire “Core Themes” vs repeated themes in “Emotional + Thematic Rulebook”   | Both sections list mood and themes (dread, resignation, climate grief, etc.). | Consolidate all thematic rules into a single section under one clear header. |
| “FX cues” references in Sonic Lore Insertion & Instrumentation & FX Layering | FX mapping instructions appear twice (SLI Protocol vs FX Layering details).   | Merge into one FX Cues & Layering subsection.                                |

### 2. Conflicts & Ambiguities

| Location                                                                                        | Conflict/Ambiguity                                                       | Resolution Needed                                                           |
| ----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------ | --------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Vocal Profile: “No hooks—only warnings” vs Lyrical Structure: “Cyclical refrains”               | Does repetition count as a “hook”? Potential misinterpretation.          | Clarify: define “hook” operationally (e.g., refrain ≠ hook).                |
| Production Style: “No prominent percussion” vs Sonic Aesthetic: “Slow to mid-tempo (50–85 BPM)” | Tempo implies rhythm; lacking percussion may conflict with BPM guidance. | Specify that pulse is implied via analog throb, not traditional percussion. |

### 3. Terminology Standardization

* Use **FX cues** consistently; remove alternate phrasing like “Background spoken FX.”
* Standardize brackets for echo syntax: use `[[echo]]` rather than Markdown-style links.
* Align “scene-based transitions” phrasing with CORE WORKFLOW conventions.

### 4. Structural Reorganization

1. **Header Block:** Origin, Version, Date.
2. **Unified Themes:** Merge Core Themes + Emotional Rulebook.
3. **Vocal Architecture:** Combine Vocal Profile + Vocal Architecture.
4. **Instrumentation & FX:** Integrate Production Style + Instrumentation & FX Layering + SLI Protocol.
5. **Lyrical & Narrative Rules:** Lyrical Structure + Intended Impact + Socio-Political Context.
6. **Application Notes:** Artifact mindset, tempo, mix concepts.
7. **Canonization & Metadata:** Location to add, Version history.
