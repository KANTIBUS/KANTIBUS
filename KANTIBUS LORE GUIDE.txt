## 4. Sound & FX Design

* **FX Brackets:** Use single brackets `[]` for all FX cues.
* **Echo Sections:** Tag with `[echo]` followed by lyrics in `()` until a new `[]` effect.
* **Instrumental FX:** Use genre-appropriate sound names (e.g., `[wooden blocks]`, `[vinyl crackle]`).
* **No FX in Lyrics Parentheses:** Never place FX or style instructions inside `()` to avoid spoken artifacts.
